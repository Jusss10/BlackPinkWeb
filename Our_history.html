First of all, they are very talented. The girls can sing, rap, dance, and perform very well. They have been training for so many years already and now their skills are very superb. In addition, their fashion styles are on point. BLACKPINK always wears the chicest and latest fashionable trend pieces when they are at the airports or fansigns. These clothes are from affordable brands up to luxury brands, many styles that BLINKS can follow up.

Most girlgroups lean towards the cute and girly style, dancing while BlackPink leans more towards a girlcrush and hiphop concept, which is quite rare but executed beautifully! But apart from these major qualities, by searching up on them, you will find out that no matter how fierce they look on stage, they are still just a bunch of cute girls improving day by day, living their dream.
